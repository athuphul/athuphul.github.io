A bit about me

I'm a software engineer at Roblox, working on distributed data problems that power one of the world's largest social platforms. I'm originally from Pune, India — where I fronted a band called loco as lead singer and rhythm guitarist before shipping off to Texas. I've been playing guitar and trained in Indian classical vocals since I was young. Music is just part of how I'm wired.

I approach most things in life the same way I approach a poker hand — read the situation carefully, manage your risk, and don't be afraid to make the big bet when the math is right. Whether that's a career move, a watch auction, or your friends convincing you that you can send it down a double black diamond on the mountain.

When I'm not writing code or at the felt, you'll find me ogling Porsches, obsessing over seconds complications, or explaining why Brighton & Hove Albion and Indian cricket are objectively correct life choices. I also ski, play rec soccer, and am currently figuring out which grip to use on a tennis racket.

Beyond the code

What I'm actually into

Work is what I do. These are what I am.

🏎️
Porsche & the art of ICE
The 987 Boxster and Cayman are the most underrated sports cars Porsche ever built — a mid-engine layout, a flat-six that screams, and a chassis that will teach you more about driving than almost anything else. I have strong opinions about the 996 being underrated, Alfa Romeos being criminally underappreciated in America, and why the sound of a flat-six at redline is one of life's great pleasures. Vespas count too.
⌚
Horology
Mechanical watchmaking is engineering as art. I appreciate the craft behind a well-executed in-house movement more than any smartwatch will ever make me feel. My grail? The Breguet Retrograde Seconds 7097 — if not for a certain British spy's influence pushing me firmly toward the Omega Seamaster instead.
🃏
Poker
No-limit Texas Hold'em, late nights, long sessions. Equal parts math, psychology, and patience. Any game is fun no matter the stakes — whether it's a tournament final table in Vegas or a weekend home game with your buddies and some cold ones. The table doesn't lie either way.
🎸
Music
Big John Mayer fan — the blues playing, the songwriting, all of it. Music is the one hobby that goes everywhere with me.
